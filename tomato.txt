# TOMATO CARE GUIDE 🍅🌿  
(High-energy fruiting vegetable — Precision nutrition plant)

---

## 🍅 Plant Nature & Behaviour Model

Tomato is a **high-speed, high-demand fruiting plant**.

Core traits:

✔ Extremely heavy feeder ⚡  
✔ Rapid growth cycles  
✔ Fruits continuously  
✔ Sensitive to nutrient imbalance  
✔ Sensitive to watering mistakes  
✔ Highly pest-prone 🐛  
✔ Highly disease-prone 🍄  
✔ Requires structural pruning/support  
✔ Root-zone stability critical

Critical mental model:

Leaves build energy → Energy builds flowers → Flowers build fruits

Wrong nutrition → Leaves only OR fruit failure

---

## ☀️ LIGHT REQUIREMENT (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

Tomatoes LOVE sun.

✔ Minimum 6–8 hrs direct sunlight ☀️  
✔ Full sun ideal

Low light symptoms:

✔ Weak stems  
✔ Few flowers ❌  
✔ Poor fruiting  
✔ Leggy plant  
✔ Disease explosion

Sunlight = Fruit engine.

---

## 🌱 SOIL MANAGEMENT (CRITICAL FOR TOMATOES)

Tomato roots are aggressive & hungry.

Ideal soil behaviour:

✔ Loose & aerated  
✔ Excellent drainage  
✔ Rich organic matter  
✔ Moisture-retentive but NOT soggy

Poor soil = Guaranteed poor yield.

---

### ✅ ORGANIC FOUNDATION (VERY IMPORTANT)

Before planting / Every 20–30 days:

✔ Vermicompost → Generous mixing  
✔ Cow Manure → Moderate mixing

Why:

✔ Tomatoes are heavy nutrient extractors  
✔ Soil depletion extremely fast

Optional booster:

✔ Humic Acid Drench

Dose:
2 g/L

Frequency:
Every 20–30 days

Why:

✔ Root efficiency  
✔ Nutrient uptake boost

---

## 💧 WATERING STRATEGY (MOST CRITICAL RULE)

Golden rule:

✔ Deep watering ✅  
✔ Stable rhythm ✅  
✔ NEVER dry–flood cycles ❌  
✔ NEVER constant soggy soil ❌

Typical rhythm:

✔ Daily light watering OR alternate day (depends on pot/weather)

Critical insight:

Water instability = MOST COMMON tomato failure.

---

### 🚨 FAILURE PATTERN

Dry → Flood → Dry → Flood ❌

Causes:

✔ Fruit cracking  
✔ Blossom end rot  
✔ Flower drop  
✔ Leaf curl  
✔ Stress growth

Consistency = Fruit quality.

---

## 🧪 FERTILIZER PROGRAM (HIGH PRECISION REQUIRED)

Tomato = Heavy feeder + Continuous fruiter.

---

### 🌿 EARLY VEGETATIVE PHASE

✔ NPK 19:19:19

Dose:
1 g/L

Frequency:
Every 7–10 days

Why:

✔ Leaf & stem development  
✔ Root expansion  
✔ Structural growth

---

### 🌼 FLOWERING PHASE (CRITICAL SHIFT)

Reduce nitrogen dominance → Increase potassium

✔ SOP 00:00:50 (MOST IMPORTANT)

Dose:
1 g/L

Frequency:
Every 10–14 days

Why:

✔ Flower formation  
✔ Fruit set  
✔ Fruit size  
✔ Taste & colour  
✔ Stress tolerance

MOST COMMON TOMATO FAILURE:

❌ Excess nitrogen → Leaves ↑ Fruits ↓

---

### 🦴 CALCIUM SUPPORT (ABSOLUTELY CRITICAL)

✔ Calcium Nitrate

Dose:
1 g/L

Frequency:
Every 10–14 days

Why:

✔ Prevents Blossom End Rot 🚨  
✔ Fruit firmness  
✔ Cell wall stability

Blossom End Rot = Calcium transport failure.

---

## 🚨 BLOSSOM END ROT (VERY IMPORTANT)

Symptoms:

✔ Black patch at fruit bottom  
✔ Fruit collapse

Cause:

✔ Calcium deficiency OR  
✔ Water instability preventing calcium transport

Fix:

✔ Calcium nitrate + Stable watering

---

### 🌿 MAGNESIUM BOOST (IMPORTANT)

✔ Epsom Salt

Dose:
0.5 – 1 g/L

Frequency:
Every 15–20 days

Why:

✔ Deep green leaves  
✔ Better photosynthesis  
✔ Fruit quality support

---

### 🔬 MICRONUTRIENTS (VERY IMPORTANT)

✔ Micronutrient Mix

Frequency:
Every 20–30 days

Why:

✔ Prevents rapid deficiencies  
✔ Improves fruiting consistency

Deficiency symptoms:

✔ Yellow leaves  
✔ Poor fruit set  
✔ Deformed growth

---

### 🌊 SEAWEED EXTRACT (EXCELLENT)

Dose:
2–3 ml/L

Frequency:
Every 15–20 days

Why:

✔ Flower stimulation  
✔ Stress buffering  
✔ Fruit retention  
✔ Root vigor

---

## ✂️ PRUNING & STRUCTURAL MANAGEMENT (CRITICAL)

Tomato ≠ Let-it-grow plant.

---

## 🌿 SUCKER MANAGEMENT (VERY IMPORTANT)

Suckers = Shoots between stem & branch.

If unchecked:

✔ Dense jungle  
✔ Small fruits  
✔ Poor airflow  
✔ Disease explosion

---

### ✅ CORRECT STRATEGY

✔ Remove most suckers  
✔ Maintain 1–3 main stems  
✔ Improve airflow

Exception:

Bush/determinate types → Minimal pruning

---

## 🌿 LOWER LEAF REMOVAL

✔ Remove lower touching-soil leaves

Why:

✔ Prevent fungal spread  
✔ Improve airflow  
✔ Reduce disease

---

## 🌿 SUPPORT STRUCTURE (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

✔ Stake / cage / trellis required

Without support:

✔ Stem breakage  
✔ Fruit damage  
✔ Disease risk

---

## 🐛 PEST MANAGEMENT (TOMATO = PEST MAGNET)

Common pests:

• Aphids  
• Whiteflies  
• Caterpillars 🐛  
• Thrips  
• Spider mites  
• Fruit borers 🚨  

---

## 🚨 FRUIT BORER (MAJOR ENEMY)

Symptoms:

✔ Hole in fruit  
✔ Internal damage  
✔ Rotting fruits

---

### ✅ PREVENTIVE BASELINE (ESSENTIAL)

✔ Neem Oil

Dose:
3–5 ml/L + Wetting agent

Frequency:
Every 7–10 days

---

### ✅ CHEWING / BORER CONTROL

✔ Chlorantraniliprole (EXCELLENT)  
✔ Indoxacarb  

Rotate chemistry.

---

### ✅ SAP SUCKING PESTS

✔ Thiamethoxam (if severe)

Neem preferred when possible.

---

## 🍄 DISEASE MANAGEMENT (CRITICAL FOR TOMATOES)

Common diseases:

✔ Early blight  
✔ Late blight  
✔ Leaf spot  
✔ Powdery mildew  
✔ Wilt diseases  

Tomatoes are disease magnets.

---

### ✅ PREVENTIVE FUNGICIDE (CRITICAL)

✔ Hexaconazole

Frequency:
Every 15–20 days (humid/rainy season)

---

### ✅ STRONG PROTECTION / CURATIVE

✔ Azoxystrobin + Mancozeb

Use when:

✔ Visible spotting  
✔ Rainy stress  
✔ Persistent fungal pressure

---

## 🍅 COMMON TOMATO PROBLEMS DECODED

Lots of leaves, few fruits → Excess nitrogen  
Flowers dropping → Heat / water stress / low potassium  
Small fruits → Potassium deficiency  
Black fruit bottom → Blossom End Rot 🚨  
Fruit cracking → Water instability 💧  
Yellow leaves → Nutrient deficiency / root stress  
Leaf curl → Stress / pests / watering imbalance  
Weak stems → Low nutrition / calcium deficiency  
Tall thin plant → Low sunlight ☀️  
Holes in fruit → Fruit borer 🐛  

---

## ⚠️ HIGH-IMPACT FAILURE POINTS

Tomatoes struggle mostly from:

1) Excess nitrogen (MOST COMMON)  
2) Calcium deficiency / transport failure  
3) Water instability  
4) Poor sunlight  
5) No pruning → Jungle plant  
6) Fruit borer damage  
7) No fungicide prevention  
8) Nutrient starvation  
9) Poor airflow

---
