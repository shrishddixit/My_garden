# GARLIC GREENS CARE GUIDE 🧄🌿  
(Garlic leaves / tender shoots — Leaf Harvest Crop)

---

## 🧄 Plant Nature & Behaviour Model

Garlic greens are a **leaf-harvest crop**, NOT bulb-focused production.

Core traits:

✔ Slow but steady growth  
✔ Low-to-moderate feeder  
✔ Shallow root system  
✔ Sensitive to overwatering ❌  
✔ Highly tolerant of cool conditions  
✔ Leaf quality nutrition-driven  
✔ Very low pest pressure (generally)  
✔ Simple management plant

Critical mental model:

Stable moisture + Light feeding = Tender greens 🌿

Overfeeding / Overwatering = Soft rot problems ❌

---

## ☀️ LIGHT REQUIREMENT

Ideal exposure:

✔ 3–5 hrs sunlight ☀️  
✔ Bright light remainder of day ✅  
✔ Mild sun preferred  

Garlic greens tolerate:

✔ Partial shade  
✔ Cooler environments

Excess harsh heat:

✔ Leaf thinning  
✔ Growth slowdown

---

## 🌱 SOIL MANAGEMENT

Ideal soil behaviour:

✔ Loose & aerated  
✔ Excellent drainage (CRITICAL)  
✔ Moderately rich  
✔ Slight moisture retention

BIGGEST GARLIC FAILURE:

❌ Wet soil → Root rot → Base softening

---

### ✅ ORGANIC FOUNDATION (LIGHT)

Before planting / Every 30–40 days:

✔ Vermicompost → Light mixing  
✔ Cow Manure → Very light mixing

Why light feeding:

✔ Garlic greens are NOT heavy feeders  
✔ Excess nutrients unnecessary

Optional booster:

✔ Humic Acid Drench

Dose:
2 g/L

Frequency:
Every 30–40 days (optional)

---

## 💧 WATERING STRATEGY (CRITICAL)

Golden rule:

✔ Soil slightly moist ✅  
✔ NEVER soggy ❌  
✔ NEVER bone dry ❌

Typical rhythm:

✔ Light watering every 2–4 days

Critical insight:

Garlic tolerates mild dryness FAR better than excess moisture.

Overwatering = Most common mistake.

---

### 🚨 SYMPTOMS DECODER

Overwatering:

✔ Yellowing leaves  
✔ Soft base  
✔ Rot smell  
✔ Sudden collapse

Underwatering:

✔ Leaf droop  
✔ Slight thinning  
✔ Usually recovers fast

Underwatering safer.

---

## 🧪 FERTILIZER PROGRAM (MINIMALIST)

Garlic greens = Low feeder.

---

### 🌿 PRIMARY FEEDING (LIGHT)

✔ NPK 19:19:19

Dose:
0.5 g/L

Frequency:
Every 15–20 days

Why:

✔ Leaf colour  
✔ Growth stability  
✔ Tender leaf quality

Heavy feeding unnecessary.

---

### 🌿 SOP (OPTIONAL)

✔ Dose → 0.5 g/L  
✔ Frequency → Every 30–40 days

Why:

✔ Stress tolerance  
✔ Leaf structure balance

Low priority.

---

### 🌿 MAGNESIUM BOOST (BENEFICIAL)

✔ Epsom Salt

Dose:
0.5 g/L

Frequency:
Every 30 days

Why:

✔ Deep green leaves  
✔ Photosynthesis support

---

### 🔬 MICRONUTRIENTS (OPTIONAL)

✔ Micronutrient Mix

Frequency:
Every 30–45 days (optional)

Garlic usually tolerant of mild deficiencies.

---

### 🌊 SEAWEED EXTRACT (EXCELLENT)

Dose:
2 ml/L

Frequency:
Every 20–30 days

Why:

✔ Root vigor  
✔ Leaf quality  
✔ Stress buffering

---

## ✂️ HARVESTING STRATEGY (VERY IMPORTANT)

Garlic greens reward controlled harvesting.

---

### ✅ CORRECT METHOD

✔ Cut outer leaves first ✂️  
✔ Allow central shoots to grow  
✔ Avoid cutting entire plant bald ❌

---

### ✅ BENEFITS OF GRADUAL HARVEST

✔ Continuous greens 🌿  
✔ Better flavour  
✔ Stable regrowth  
✔ Prevents stress shock

---

### 🚨 COMMON MISTAKE

❌ Aggressive cutting → Growth slowdown

---

## 🐛 PEST MANAGEMENT (LOW RISK)

Garlic naturally pest-resistant.

Rare pests:

✔ Aphids (occasionally)  
✔ Thrips (rare)  

---

### ✅ PREVENTIVE BASELINE

✔ Neem Oil

Dose:
3 ml/L

Frequency:
Only if pests seen OR once monthly preventive

Excess spraying unnecessary.

---

## 🍄 DISEASE MANAGEMENT (RARE)

Major disease = Root rot.

Cause:

❌ Overwatering  
❌ Poor drainage

Fungicides rarely needed if watering corrected.

---

## 🧄 COMMON GARLIC GREEN PROBLEMS DECODED

Yellow leaves → Overwatering 💧  
Thin weak leaves → Low nutrition  
Slow growth → Nutrient-poor soil  
Soft base → Root rot ❌  
Leaf droop → Underwatering  
Poor colour → Magnesium deficiency  
Weak regrowth → Aggressive harvesting  
Patchy leaves → Rare nutrient imbalance  

---

## ⚠️ HIGH-IMPACT FAILURE POINTS

Garlic greens struggle mostly from:

1) Overwatering (MOST COMMON FAILURE) 🚨  
2) Poor drainage  
3) Excess fertilization  
4) Aggressive harvesting  
5) Severe nutrient starvation  
6) Heat stress (extreme conditions)

---

## 🎯 OPTIMAL CARE PHILOSOPHY

Garlic greens thrive on:

✔ Well-draining loose soil 🌱  
✔ Light nutrition strategy 🧪  
✔ Stable mild moisture 💧  
✔ Moderate sunlight ☀️  
✔ Occasional magnesium support  
✔ Periodic micronutrients 🔬  
✔ Seaweed root support 🌊  
✔ Gentle harvesting ✂️  
✔ Minimal intervention  
✔ Good airflow 🌬  

Garlic greens are low-drama plants 😄

Too much care → Problems  
Correct restraint → Beautiful greens 🌿
