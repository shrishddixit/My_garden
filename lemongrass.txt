# LEMONGRASS CARE GUIDE 🌿🍃  
(Cymbopogon citratus — Aromatic Grass / Leaf Harvest Plant)

---

## 🌿 Plant Nature & Behaviour Model

Lemongrass is a **leaf-harvest aromatic grass**, not a delicate herb.

Core traits:

✔ Extremely hardy  
✔ Loves sunlight ☀️  
✔ Loves heat 🔥  
✔ Moderate feeder  
✔ VERY drought tolerant once established  
✔ Hates waterlogging ❌  
✔ Aggressive root system  
✔ Responds strongly to pruning/harvesting ✂️  
✔ Fast regrowth plant

Critical mental model:

Lemongrass behaves like an energy factory.

Cut it → It grows more  
Pamper it → It rots

---

## ☀️ LIGHT REQUIREMENT (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

Lemongrass LOVES sun.

✔ Minimum 6–8 hrs direct sunlight  
✔ Full sun ideal

Low light symptoms:

✔ Thin weak blades  
✔ Poor aroma  
✔ Slow growth  
✔ Pale green colour

Sunlight = Aroma engine.

---

## 🌱 SOIL MANAGEMENT (CRITICAL)

Ideal soil behaviour:

✔ Fast draining (CRITICAL)  
✔ Loose & aerated  
✔ Never compacted  
✔ Moderate organic matter

BIGGEST LEMONGRASS KILLER:

❌ Wet soggy soil → Root rot → Sudden collapse

---

### ✅ ORGANIC FOUNDATION

Every 30–45 days:

✔ Vermicompost → 200–300 g  
✔ Cow Manure → Light handful

Why:

✔ Sustains leaf density  
✔ Improves aroma intensity  
✔ Supports regrowth cycles

Optional booster:

✔ Humic Acid Drench

Dose:
2 g/L

Frequency:
Every 30–45 days

Why:

✔ Root vigor  
✔ Nutrient absorption boost

---

## 💧 WATERING STRATEGY (VERY IMPORTANT)

Golden rule:

✔ Allow drying between watering  
✔ Deep watering preferred  
✔ NEVER constant moisture

Typical rhythm:

✔ Every 3–5 days (heat dependent)

Critical insight:

Lemongrass tolerates drought FAR better than excess water.

Classic failure:

✘ Frequent watering ❌

Symptoms of overwatering:

✔ Yellowing blades  
✔ Weak clumps  
✔ Root rot  
✔ Mushy base

Symptoms of underwatering:

✔ Slight blade folding (rarely serious)

Underwatering safer.

---

## 🧪 FERTILIZER PROGRAM (LEAF PRODUCTION FOCUSED)

Lemongrass = Moderate feeder.

---

### 🌿 PRIMARY FEEDING

✔ NPK 19:19:19

Dose:
0.5 – 1 g/L

Frequency:
Every 10–14 days

Why:

✔ Blade density  
✔ Faster regrowth  
✔ Aroma quality  
✔ Clump vigor

Nitrogen drives leafy growth.

---

### 🌿 SOP (SUPPORTIVE ONLY)

✔ Dose → 0.5 g/L  
✔ Frequency → Every 30–45 days

Why:

✔ Stress tolerance  
✔ Structural balance

Too much potassium unnecessary.

---

### 🌿 MAGNESIUM BOOST (IMPORTANT)

✔ Epsom Salt

Dose:
0.5 – 1 g/L

Frequency:
Every 30 days

Why:

✔ Deep green blades  
✔ Better photosynthesis  
✔ Aroma quality

---

### 🔬 MICRONUTRIENTS (OPTIONAL BUT BENEFICIAL)

✔ Micronutrient Mix

Frequency:
Every 30–45 days

Why:

✔ Sustains long-term vigor  
✔ Prevents chlorosis

---

### 🌊 SEAWEED EXTRACT (EXCELLENT)

Dose:
2 ml/L

Frequency:
Every 20–30 days

Why:

✔ Stress buffering  
✔ Root vigor  
✔ Faster recovery after cutting

---

## ✂️ PRUNING / HARVESTING STRATEGY (VERY IMPORTANT)

Harvesting = Growth stimulation.

Lemongrass LOVES cutting.

---

### ✅ WHEN TO HARVEST

✔ Anytime clump dense  
✔ When blades mature  
✔ When aroma strong

---

### ✅ CORRECT CUTTING METHOD

✔ Cut entire stalks near base  
✔ Use sharp scissors/knife  
✔ Avoid tearing

---

### ✅ GOLDEN RULE

✔ Do NOT cut entire plant bald ❌  
✔ Leave ~30–40% foliage

Ensures rapid regrowth.

---

### ✅ BENEFIT OF REGULAR CUTTING

✔ More shoots  
✔ Denser clump  
✔ Stronger aroma  
✔ Healthier plant

Neglected lemongrass becomes woody & messy.

---

## 🐛 PEST MANAGEMENT

Lemongrass is NATURALLY pest-resistant.

Rare pests:

✔ Aphids (rare)  
✔ Mealybugs (rare)  
✔ Mites (rare)

---

### ✅ PREVENTIVE BASELINE

✔ Neem Oil

Dose:
3 ml/L

Frequency:
Only if pests seen OR once monthly preventive

Avoid excessive spraying.

---

## 🍄 DISEASE MANAGEMENT

Major disease = Root rot.

Cause:

❌ Overwatering  
❌ Poor drainage

Fungicides rarely needed if watering corrected.

---

## 🌿 COMMON LEMONGRASS PROBLEMS DECODED

Yellow blades → Overwatering 💧  
Weak aroma → Low sunlight / Low nutrition ☀️  
Thin blades → Nitrogen deficiency 🌿  
Slow regrowth → Nutrient depletion / Root stress  
Mushy base → Root rot ❌  
Woody stalks → No harvesting ✂️  
Sparse clump → Needs pruning/division  

---

## ⚠️ HIGH-IMPACT FAILURE POINTS

Lemongrass struggles mostly from:

1) Overwatering (MOST COMMON)  
2) Poor drainage  
3) Low sunlight  
4) No harvesting  
5) Nutrient starvation  
6) Compacted soil

---

## 🎯 OPTIMAL CARE PHILOSOPHY

Lemongrass thrives on:

✔ Full sunlight ☀️  
✔ Excellent drainage 🌱  
✔ Deep spaced watering 💧  
✔ Moderate nitrogen feeding 🌿  
✔ Occasional magnesium support  
✔ Periodic micronutrients 🔬  
✔ Regular harvesting ✂️  
✔ Warm heat exposure 🔥  
✔ Minimal fuss

Lemongrass is a warrior plant.

Treat it tough → It thrives  
Treat it delicate → It rots
