# LEMON — SMALL FRUIT DROP 🍋  
Classic Citrus Puzzle Decoded

First reassuring truth:

This is one of the MOST COMMON citrus behaviours.

Your tree is not “failing”.

It is performing **physiological fruit thinning** — but possibly exaggerated by stress.

Let’s dissect the real causes.

---

## 🌼 WHY LEMON TREES DROP TINY FRUITS

Fruit formation is metabolically expensive.

Tree evaluates:

“Do I have enough resources to raise all these babies?”

If answer = No → Abort mission → Fruit drop.

This is survival intelligence, not malfunction.

---

## ✅ MOST COMMON TRIGGERS

---

### 1️⃣ POTASSIUM DEFICIENCY (MOST LIKELY)

Potassium = Fruit retention hormone regulator.

Low potassium → Tiny fruits detach early.

Symptoms:

✔ Heavy flowering ✅  
✔ Fruit initiation ✅  
✔ Tiny fruit drop ❌

Tree behaviour:

“Flowers are cheap. Fruits are expensive.”

Fix (CRITICAL):

✔ SOP 00:00:50 → Every 10–15 days  
Dose → 1 g/L

This is your #1 corrective lever.

---

### 2️⃣ WATER STRESS (EXTREMELY COMMON)

Both extremes trigger fruit abortion.

Underwatering:

✔ Tree enters conservation mode  
✔ Drops fruits first

Overwatering:

✔ Roots stressed  
✔ Oxygen deprivation  
✔ Fruit drop

Key insight:

Even brief stress episodes cause drop.

Correct pattern:

✔ Deep watering  
✔ Allow slight drying  
✔ Stable rhythm

NOT:

✘ Dry → Flood → Dry → Flood

---

### 3️⃣ CALCIUM DEFICIENCY (VERY COMMON)

Calcium = Structural stability for young fruits.

Low Ca → Weak fruit attachment.

Symptoms:

✔ Fruits form  
✔ Drop early  
✔ Sometimes deformed

Fix:

✔ Calcium Nitrate → Every 25–30 days  
Dose → 1 g/L

Do NOT mix with SOP.

Alternate cycles.

---

### 4️⃣ EXCESS FRUIT LOAD (NATURAL BEHAVIOUR)

Tree produces MANY flowers intentionally.

Most fruits are sacrificed.

Normal drop rate:

✔ 70–90% is biologically normal

Abnormal drop:

✔ Nearly ALL fruits falling

---

### 5️⃣ TEMPERATURE / HEAT STRESS

Common in Bangalore.

High heat → Hormonal imbalance → Drop.

Seaweed extract helps stabilize stress response.

✔ Seaweed → Every 20–30 days

---

### 6️⃣ MICRONUTRIENT DEFICIENCY (SILENT FACTOR)

Especially:

✔ Boron (VERY IMPORTANT)  
✔ Zinc

Low micronutrients → Fruit set failure.

Fix:

✔ Micronutrient Mix → Every 30–45 days

---

### 7️⃣ ROOT HEALTH ISSUES

Hidden root stress = Classic fruit drop cause.

Indicators:

✔ Yellow leaves  
✔ Leaf drop  
✔ Slow growth  
✔ Wilting despite watering

Most common culprit:

✘ Overwatering / poor drainage

---
