# GREEN CHILLI CARE GUIDE 🌶🌿  
(Capsicum species — Continuous Fruiting Plant)

---

## 🌶 Plant Nature & Behaviour Model

Green chilli is a **long-duration fruiting plant**, very different from tomato.

Core traits:

✔ Hardy & forgiving  
✔ Continuous flowering & fruiting  
✔ Moderate-to-heavy feeder  
✔ Loves sunlight ☀️  
✔ Very pruning responsive ✂️  
✔ Sensitive to overwatering ❌  
✔ Strong potassium responder  
✔ Calcium important for fruit quality  
✔ Highly pest-prone 🐛  

Critical mental model:

More branches → More flowers → More chillies 🌶

Structure directly defines yield.

---

## ☀️ LIGHT REQUIREMENT (CRITICAL)

Chilli LOVES sun.

✔ Minimum 6–8 hrs direct sunlight ☀️  
✔ Full sun ideal

Low light symptoms:

✔ Few flowers ❌  
✔ Weak stems  
✔ Long leggy growth  
✔ Small fruits  
✔ Poor yield

Sunlight = Fruit engine.

---

## 🌱 SOIL MANAGEMENT (IMPORTANT)

Ideal soil behaviour:

✔ Loose & aerated  
✔ Excellent drainage (CRITICAL)  
✔ Rich organic matter  
✔ Moisture-retentive but NOT soggy

BIGGEST CHILLI KILLER:

❌ Wet soil → Root rot → Flower drop → Yield collapse

---

### ✅ ORGANIC FOUNDATION

Every 30–40 days:

✔ Vermicompost → 200–300 g  
✔ Cow Manure → Light handful

Why:

✔ Sustains long-term fruiting  
✔ Supports microbial health  
✔ Improves soil vitality

Optional booster:

✔ Humic Acid Drench

Dose:
2 g/L

Frequency:
Every 30 days

Why:

✔ Root efficiency  
✔ Better nutrient uptake

---

## 💧 WATERING STRATEGY (MOST CRITICAL RULE)

Golden rule:

✔ Deep watering ✅  
✔ Allow slight drying ✅  
✔ NEVER constant wet soil ❌

Typical rhythm:

✔ Every 2–4 days (depends on heat & pot size)

Critical insight:

Chilli tolerates mild drought FAR better than excess water.

Overwatering = Flower killer.

---

### 🚨 SYMPTOMS DECODER

Overwatering:

✔ Yellow leaves  
✔ Leaf drop  
✔ Flower drop 🌼❌  
✔ Weak growth  
✔ Root rot

Underwatering:

✔ Leaf droop  
✔ Temporary stress (usually recovers)

Underwatering safer.

---

## 🧪 FERTILIZER PROGRAM (FRUITING DOMINANT)

Chilli = Moderate-to-heavy feeder.

---

### 🌿 EARLY VEGETATIVE PHASE

✔ NPK 19:19:19

Dose:
0.5 – 1 g/L

Frequency:
Every 10–14 days

Why:

✔ Leaf & branch development  
✔ Root expansion  
✔ Structural growth

---

### 🌼 FLOWERING & FRUITING PHASE (MOST IMPORTANT)

✔ SOP 00:00:50 (CRITICAL)

Dose:
1 g/L

Frequency:
Every 10–14 days

Why:

✔ Flower formation 🌼  
✔ Fruit set 🌶  
✔ Fruit size  
✔ Pungency & flavour  
✔ Stress tolerance

MOST COMMON CHILLI FAILURE:

❌ Excess nitrogen → Leaves ↑ Flowers ↓

Potassium = Yield stabilizer.

---

### 🦴 CALCIUM SUPPORT (VERY IMPORTANT)

✔ Calcium Nitrate

Dose:
0.5 – 1 g/L

Frequency:
Every 15–20 days

Why:

✔ Prevents fruit deformities  
✔ Strong stems  
✔ Better fruit quality  
✔ Prevents blossom-end issues

Do NOT mix with SOP.

Alternate cycles.

---

### 🌿 MAGNESIUM BOOST (IMPORTANT)

✔ Epsom Salt

Dose:
0.5 – 1 g/L

Frequency:
Every 20–30 days

Why:

✔ Deep green leaves  
✔ Better photosynthesis  
✔ Fruit quality support

---

### 🔬 MICRONUTRIENTS (IMPORTANT)

✔ Micronutrient Mix

Frequency:
Every 30 days

Why:

✔ Sustains flowering cycles  
✔ Prevents deficiencies  
✔ Improves fruit set

Deficiency symptoms:

✔ Flower drop 🌼❌  
✔ Pale leaves  
✔ Small fruits

---

### 🌊 SEAWEED EXTRACT (EXCELLENT)

Dose:
2 ml/L

Frequency:
Every 20–30 days

Why:

✔ Flower stimulation  
✔ Stress buffering  
✔ Fruit retention 🌶  
✔ Root vigor

---

## ✂️ PRUNING STRATEGY (CRITICAL FOR YIELD)

Chilli LOVES pruning.

No pruning → Tall thin plant → Low yield.

---

### ✅ OBJECTIVES

✔ Encourage branching  
✔ Increase flowering points  
✔ Maintain compact bush  
✔ Improve airflow

More branches = More chillies 🌶

---

### ✅ TIP PINCHING (VERY IMPORTANT)

✔ Pinch early growth tips

Why:

✔ Stimulates lateral branching  
✔ Prevents tall weak plant

---

### ✅ WHAT TO REMOVE

✔ Weak thin stems  
✔ Damaged leaves  
✔ Excess inner growth  
✔ Yellow leaves

---

### ✅ PRUNING INTENSITY

✔ Moderate pruning VERY beneficial  
✔ Hard pruning tolerated by mature plants

---

## 🌶 FLOWERING FAILURE INSIGHTS

Flowers dropping → MOST COMMON causes:

✔ Overwatering 💧  
✔ Heat stress ☀️  
✔ Potassium deficiency 🧪  
✔ Nutrient imbalance  
✔ Pest stress 🐛  
✔ Micronutrient deficiency 🔬  

---

## 🐛 PEST MANAGEMENT (CHILLI = PEST MAGNET)

Common pests:

• Aphids  
• Thrips 🚨  
• Spider mites 🕷  
• Whiteflies  
• Mealybugs  
• Fruit borers 🐛  

---

## 🚨 THRIPS (VERY COMMON)

Symptoms:

✔ Leaf curling  
✔ Silver streaks  
✔ Flower drop 🌼❌  
✔ Deformed fruits

---

### ✅ PREVENTIVE BASELINE (ESSENTIAL)

✔ Neem Oil

Dose:
3–5 ml/L + Wetting agent

Frequency:
Every 7–10 days

Neem VERY effective for chilli pests.

---

### ✅ SAP SUCKING PESTS

✔ Thiamethoxam (if severe)

Use only when needed.

---

### ✅ CHEWING / BORER CONTROL

✔ Chlorantraniliprole (EXCELLENT)  
✔ Indoxacarb  

Rotate chemistry.

---

## 🍄 DISEASE MANAGEMENT

Common diseases:

✔ Leaf spot  
✔ Powdery mildew  
✔ Wilt diseases  
✔ Root rot ❌  

---

### ✅ PREVENTIVE FUNGICIDE

✔ Hexaconazole

Frequency:
Every 20–30 days (humid/rainy season)

---

### ✅ STRONG PROTECTION

✔ Azoxystrobin + Mancozeb

Use when spotting visible.

---

## 🌶 COMMON CHILLI PROBLEMS DECODED

Lots of leaves, few chillies → Excess nitrogen  
Flower drop → Overwatering / stress / low potassium  
Small fruits → Potassium deficiency  
Leaf curl → Thrips / stress / watering imbalance  
Yellow leaves → Root stress / nutrient deficiency  
Weak stems → Calcium deficiency  
Holes in fruit → Fruit borer 🐛  
Slow growth → Nutrient depletion  
Leggy plant → Low sunlight ☀️  

---

## ⚠️ HIGH-IMPACT FAILURE POINTS

Chilli struggles mostly from:

1) Overwatering (MOST COMMON)  
2) Excess nitrogen  
3) Potassium deficiency  
4) No pruning / no branching  
5) Thrips & mites 🐛  
6) Poor sunlight  
7) Calcium deficiency  
8) Nutrient starvation  
9) Poor airflow

---

## 🎯 OPTIMAL CARE PHILOSOPHY

Green chilli thrives on:

✔ Full sunlight ☀️  
✔ Excellent drainage 🌱  
✔ Deep spaced watering 💧  
✔ Balanced NPK feeding 🌿  
✔ Potassium-dominant flowering support 🌶  
✔ Regular calcium supplementation 🦴  
✔ Magnesium stability  
✔ Periodic micronutrients 🔬  
✔ Seaweed stress buffering 🌊  
✔ Frequent tip pruning ✂️  
✔ Neem preventive shield 🐛  
✔ Good airflow 🌬  

Chilli is a structure-driven yield plant.

More branches → More flowers → More fruits 🌶

Starve structure → Starve yield.
