# ONION GREENS CARE GUIDE 🧅🌿  
(Spring Onion / Tender Leaf Harvest Crop)

---

## 🧅 Plant Nature & Behaviour Model

Onion greens are a **leaf-harvest crop**, similar philosophy to garlic greens but faster.

Core traits:

✔ Fast regrowth plant ⚡  
✔ Low-to-moderate feeder  
✔ Shallow root system  
✔ Sensitive to waterlogging ❌  
✔ Very forgiving plant 😄  
✔ Leaf quality nutrition-driven  
✔ Simple maintenance crop  
✔ Excellent for continuous harvesting ✂️

Critical mental model:

Stable moisture + Light feeding → Continuous greens 🌿

Overwatering → Root rot  
Overfeeding → Soft weak growth

---

## ☀️ LIGHT REQUIREMENT

Ideal exposure:

✔ 3–5 hrs sunlight ☀️  
✔ Bright light rest of day ✅  

Onion greens tolerate:

✔ Partial shade  
✔ Mild heat  

Excess harsh heat:

✔ Leaf thinning  
✔ Faster drying

Low light symptoms:

✔ Thin leaves  
✔ Pale colour  
✔ Slow regrowth

---

## 🌱 SOIL MANAGEMENT

Ideal soil behaviour:

✔ Loose & aerated  
✔ Excellent drainage (CRITICAL)  
✔ Moderately rich  
✔ Moisture-retentive but NOT soggy

BIGGEST FAILURE MODE:

❌ Wet soil → Root rot → Base softening

---

### ✅ ORGANIC FOUNDATION (LIGHT)

Before planting / Every 30 days:

✔ Vermicompost → Light mixing  
✔ Cow Manure → Very light mixing

Why light feeding:

✔ Greens are NOT heavy feeders  
✔ Excess organics unnecessary

Optional booster:

✔ Humic Acid Drench

Dose:
2 g/L

Frequency:
Every 30 days (optional)

---

## 💧 WATERING STRATEGY (CRITICAL)

Golden rule:

✔ Soil slightly moist ✅  
✔ NEVER soggy ❌  
✔ NEVER bone dry ❌

Typical rhythm:

✔ Light watering every 1–3 days

Critical insight:

Onion greens prefer **consistent mild moisture**.

Underwatering symptoms:

✔ Leaf droop  
✔ Growth slowdown  
✔ Leaves toughen

Overwatering symptoms:

✔ Yellow leaves  
✔ Soft base  
✔ Rotting smell

Consistency = Tender leaves.

---

## 🧪 FERTILIZER PROGRAM (MINIMALIST)

Onion greens = Low feeder.

---

### 🌿 PRIMARY FEEDING (LIGHT)

✔ NPK 19:19:19

Dose:
0.5 g/L

Frequency:
Every 15–20 days

Why:

✔ Leaf colour  
✔ Growth stability  
✔ Faster regrowth 🌿

Heavy feeding unnecessary.

---

### 🌿 SOP (OPTIONAL)

✔ Dose → 0.5 g/L  
✔ Frequency → Every 30–40 days

Why:

✔ Stress tolerance  
✔ Leaf strength

Low priority.

---

### 🌿 MAGNESIUM BOOST (BENEFICIAL)

✔ Epsom Salt

Dose:
0.5 g/L

Frequency:
Every 25–30 days

Why:

✔ Deep green leaves  
✔ Prevents yellowing

---

### 🔬 MICRONUTRIENTS (OPTIONAL)

✔ Micronutrient Mix

Frequency:
Every 30–45 days (optional)

---

### 🌊 SEAWEED EXTRACT (EXCELLENT)

Dose:
2 ml/L

Frequency:
Every 20–30 days

Why:

✔ Root vigor  
✔ Leaf thickness  
✔ Stress buffering

---

## ✂️ HARVESTING STRATEGY (VERY IMPORTANT)

Onion greens LOVE harvesting.

---

### ✅ CORRECT METHOD

✔ Cut leaves above base ✂️  
✔ Leave root system intact  
✔ Allow continuous regrowth

---

### ✅ BENEFITS OF REGULAR HARVEST

✔ Continuous greens 🌿  
✔ Faster regrowth  
✔ Prevents overcrowding  
✔ Better flavour

---

### 🚨 COMMON MISTAKE

❌ Pulling entire plant → Growth cycle ends

Treat like haircut, not uprooting 😄

---

## 🐛 PEST MANAGEMENT (LOW RISK)

Onion greens are naturally pest-resistant.

Occasional pests:

✔ Aphids  
✔ Thrips (rare)

---

### ✅ PREVENTIVE BASELINE

✔ Neem Oil

Dose:
3 ml/L

Frequency:
Only if pests seen OR once monthly preventive

---

## 🍄 DISEASE MANAGEMENT (RARE)

Major issue:

✔ Root rot (watering issue)

Fungicides rarely needed if watering corrected.

---

## 🧅 COMMON ONION GREEN PROBLEMS DECODED

Yellow leaves → Overwatering 💧  
Thin leaves → Low nutrition  
Slow regrowth → Nutrient depletion  
Leaf droop → Underwatering  
Soft base → Root rot ❌  
Poor colour → Magnesium deficiency  
Weak regrowth → Aggressive cutting at base  
Sparse growth → Soil depletion

---

## ⚠️ HIGH-IMPACT FAILURE POINTS

Onion greens struggle mostly from:

1) Overwatering (MOST COMMON FAILURE) 🚨  
2) Poor drainage  
3) Severe underwatering  
4) Nutrient starvation  
5) Aggressive harvesting damage  
6) Extreme heat stress

---

## 🎯 OPTIMAL CARE PHILOSOPHY

Onion greens thrive on:

✔ Loose well-draining soil 🌱  
✔ Stable mild moisture 💧  
✔ Light nutrition strategy 🧪  
✔ Moderate sunlight ☀️  
✔ Occasional magnesium support  
✔ Periodic micronutrients 🔬  
✔ Seaweed root support 🌊  
✔ Gentle harvesting ✂️  
✔ Minimal intervention  
✔ Good airflow 🌬  

Onion greens are wonderfully forgiving 😄

Basic care → Reliable harvest 🧅🌿
