# CHINA DOLL CARE GUIDE 🌿  
(Radermachera sinica — Ornamental Foliage Plant)

---

## 🌿 Plant Nature & Behaviour Model

China Doll is a **decorative foliage plant**, famous for beauty… and instability drama.

Core traits:

✔ Grown for leaf aesthetics  
✔ Sensitive to environmental changes  
✔ Extremely sensitive to overwatering ❌  
✔ Extremely sensitive to underwatering ❌  
✔ Loves bright indirect light  
✔ Poor recovery from stress  
✔ Pest magnet 🐛  
✔ Leaf drop prone  
✔ Moderate feeder

Critical mental model:

China Doll = Stability addict.

Small disturbances → Big reactions.

---

## ☀️ LIGHT REQUIREMENT (CRITICAL)

Ideal exposure:

✔ Bright indirect light ✅  
✔ Filtered sunlight ✅  
✔ Mild morning sun OK  
✔ Harsh direct sun ❌  
✔ Deep shade ❌

Symptoms of excess sun:

✔ Leaf burn  
✔ Yellow patches  
✔ Stress

Symptoms of low light:

✔ Leaf drop  
✔ Leggy stems  
✔ Dull foliage  
✔ Sparse canopy

Balanced bright light is essential.

---

## 🌱 SOIL MANAGEMENT (EXTREMELY IMPORTANT)

Soil MUST drain well.

Ideal soil behaviour:

✔ Loose & aerated  
✔ Fast draining  
✔ Never compacted  
✔ Moderate organic content

BIGGEST CHINA DOLL KILLER:

❌ Wet soil → Root rot → Sudden leaf drop

---

### ✅ ORGANIC FOUNDATION (LIGHT & CONTROLLED)

Every 30–45 days:

✔ Vermicompost → 200–300 g  
✔ Cow Manure → VERY LIGHT (small handful)

Why light feeding:

✔ Roots sensitive  
✔ Excess organics → Rot risk

Optional booster:

✔ Humic Acid Drench

Dose:
2 g/L

Frequency:
Every 30–45 days

Why:

✔ Root health  
✔ Nutrient absorption boost

---

## 💧 WATERING STRATEGY (MOST CRITICAL RULE)

Golden rule:

✔ Slightly moist soil  
✔ NEVER soggy  
✔ NEVER bone dry

Typical rhythm:

✔ Every 2–4 days (environment dependent)

Critical insight:

China Doll hates BOTH extremes.

Classic failure patterns:

✘ Frequent watering ❌  
✘ Forgetting watering ❌

---

### 🚨 SYMPTOMS DECODER

Overwatering:

✔ Yellow leaves  
✔ Rapid leaf drop  
✔ Soft stems  
✔ Root rot

Underwatering:

✔ Sudden leaf drop  
✔ Dry crispy leaves  
✔ Recovery slow

China Doll punishes inconsistency.

---

## 🧪 FERTILIZER PROGRAM (FOLIAGE QUALITY FOCUSED)

Moderate feeder.

---

### 🌿 PRIMARY FEEDING

✔ NPK 19:19:19

Dose:
0.5 – 1 g/L

Frequency:
Every 10–14 days

Why:

✔ Leaf density  
✔ Deep green foliage  
✔ Sustained growth  
✔ Visual fullness

Excess nitrogen rarely a problem here (leaf plant).

---

### 🌿 SOP (SUPPORTIVE ONLY)

✔ Dose → 0.5 g/L  
✔ Frequency → Every 30–45 days

Why:

✔ Stress tolerance  
✔ Structural balance

Too much potassium unnecessary.

---

### 🌿 MAGNESIUM BOOST (IMPORTANT)

✔ Epsom Salt

Dose:
0.5 – 1 g/L

Frequency:
Every 30 days

Why:

✔ Deep green leaves  
✔ Prevents chlorosis  
✔ Improves leaf texture

Magnesium deficiency common.

---

### 🔬 MICRONUTRIENTS (IMPORTANT)

✔ Micronutrient Mix

Frequency:
Every 30–45 days

Why:

✔ Prevents yellowing  
✔ Supports foliage health

Deficiency symptoms:

✔ Yellow leaves with green veins  
✔ Pale canopy

---

### 🌊 SEAWEED EXTRACT (EXCELLENT)

Dose:
2 ml/L

Frequency:
Every 20–30 days

Why:

✔ Stress buffering  
✔ Root vigor  
✔ Improves resilience

China Doll LOVES seaweed support.

---

## 🐛 PEST MANAGEMENT (CHINA DOLL = PEST MAGNET)

Common pests:

• Aphids  
• Spider mites (VERY COMMON) 🕷  
• Mealybugs  
• Whiteflies  
• Scale insects  

This plant attracts EVERYTHING.

---

## 🚨 SPIDER MITES (MOST COMMON ENEMY)

Symptoms:

✔ Tiny yellow speckles  
✔ Leaf dullness  
✔ Fine webbing  
✔ Rapid leaf drop

Cause:

✔ Dry heat + poor airflow

---

### ✅ PREVENTIVE BASELINE (ESSENTIAL)

✔ Neem Oil

Dose:
3–5 ml/L + Wetting agent

Frequency:
Every 7–10 days

YES — frequent prevention needed.

---

### ✅ SAP SUCKING PESTS

✔ Thiamethoxam (if infestation visible)

Use only when necessary.

---

### ✅ MITES / PERSISTENT ISSUES

✔ Rotate chemistry (Indoxacarb etc.)

Neem consistency often sufficient.

---

## 🍄 DISEASE MANAGEMENT

Most disease issues stem from:

❌ Root stress  
❌ Overwatering  
❌ Poor airflow

Common:

✔ Root rot  
✔ Leaf spot (rare)

---

### ✅ PREVENTIVE

✔ Hexaconazole

Frequency:
Every 30–45 days (humid/rainy season)

---

### ✅ CURATIVE

✔ Azoxystrobin + Mancozeb

Use when spotting visible.

---

## ✂️ PRUNING STRATEGY (IMPORTANT FOR STRUCTURE)

China Doll benefits from light pruning.

---

### ✅ WHEN TO PRUNE

✔ When leggy  
✔ When sparse  
✔ For shape correction

---

### ✅ PRUNING OBJECTIVES

✔ Encourage branching  
✔ Increase density  
✔ Maintain compact structure

---

### ✅ WHAT TO REMOVE

✔ Weak thin stems  
✔ Long leggy branches  
✔ Damaged leaves  
✔ Dead growth

---

### ✅ CUTTING TECHNIQUE

✔ Cut above leaf node  
✔ Slight angled cut  
✔ Clean sharp pruner  
✔ Avoid heavy pruning shock

China Doll dislikes aggressive cutting.

---

## 🌿 COMMON CHINA DOLL PROBLEMS DECODED

Sudden leaf drop → MOST COMMON causes:

✔ Watering imbalance 💧  
✔ Environmental change 🌡  
✔ Root stress 🌱  

Yellow leaves → Overwatering  
Small dull leaves → Low nutrition  
Leaf speckles → Spider mites 🕷  
Weak stems → Low light ☀️  
Slow recovery → Normal behaviour (stress sensitive)

Critical truth:

Leaf drop = Default protest mechanism.

China Doll struggles mostly from:

5) Environmental instability
